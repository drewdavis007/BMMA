Meet Our Founder

Kayla Collum Davis

DNP, FNP-C

Kayla Collum Davis is the Founder of Beautiful Minds Medical Aesthetics. She graduated from the University of Mississippi Medical Center in 2019 with her Bachelor of Science in Nursing degree. She then worked in multiple nursing specialties in the hospital setting while completing her Doctorate of Nurse Practitioner degree in Family Medicine at the University of Alabama at Birmingham in 2023.

Throughout her career, Kayla has always been passionate about two things: medical healthcare and cosmetic aesthetics. Rather than choosing one path, she decided to combine her dual passions to create something unique‚Äîa clinic that integrates both medical care and aesthetic services.

Her extensive background in family medicine gives her a comprehensive understanding of how overall health impacts appearance and well-being. This holistic perspective is what sets Beautiful Minds Medical Aesthetics apart from traditional med spas.

Kayla's philosophy is simple: beautiful skin is healthy skin, and true beauty begins with proper care from the inside out.
