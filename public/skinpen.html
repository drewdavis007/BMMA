What is Skin Pen Microneedling?

Skin Pen is the first FDA-cleared microneedling device, designed to safely and effectively treat facial acne scars, fine lines, wrinkles, and overall skin texture. This minimally invasive treatment uses fine needles to create controlled micro-injuries to the skin, triggering your body's natural wound healing process to produce new collagen and elastin.

The Science Behind Skin Pen

Microneedling works by activating your skin's natural healing response. As the tiny needles create controlled micro-channels in the skin, your body rushes to repair these areas by producing fresh collagen and elastin‚Äîthe building blocks of healthy, youthful skin. This process, called neocollagenesis, results in smoother, firmer, more radiant skin over time.

The Treatment Timeline

Week 1

Initial Healing

Immediately after treatment, you may experience redness and slight swelling, similar to a mild sunburn. This typically subsides within 24-48 hours. Your skin begins the healing process.

Week 2-4

Collagen Production Begins

Your skin starts producing new collagen. You may notice your skin feeling smoother and looking more radiant. The healing process is well underway.

Week 4-6

Visible Improvement

Skin texture continues to improve. Fine lines soften, pores appear smaller, and overall skin quality enhances. Time for your next treatment in the series!

3-6 Months

Maximum Results

After completing your treatment series, you'll see continued improvement as collagen continues to build. Results can last for months with proper skincare maintenance.

What to Expect

Before Your Treatment

  • Avoid sun exposure and tanning for 2 weeks prior
  • Discontinue retinoids and exfoliating products 3-5 days before
  • Avoid blood-thinning medications if medically safe (aspirin, ibuprofen, etc.)
  • Arrive with a clean, makeup-free face
  • Stay well-hydrated in the days leading up to treatment

During Your Treatment

  • Treatment takes approximately 30-45 minutes
  • Numbing cream is applied for 20-30 minutes before the procedure
  • The Skin Pen device is gently moved across treatment areas
  • Most patients describe the sensation as mild vibration or light scratching
  • A soothing serum is applied after treatment

After Your Treatment

  • Expect redness and mild swelling for 24-48 hours (like a sunburn)
  • Avoid makeup for 24 hours
  • Use only gentle, approved skincare products
  • Avoid direct sun exposure and wear SPF 30+ daily
  • Don't use retinoids or harsh exfoliants for 5-7 days
  • Avoid strenuous exercise for 24 hours
  • Keep skin well-moisturized and hydrated
  • Slight peeling or flaking may occur 3-5 days post-treatment (this is normal)

Frequently Asked Questions

Does it hurt?

We apply a topical numbing cream before treatment to maximize comfort. Most patients describe the sensation as tolerable, with some areas being more sensitive than others. The discomfort is brief and well worth the results!

How long is the downtime?

You'll experience redness and possibly some swelling for 24-48 hours after treatment. Most patients return to normal activities the next day, though we recommend scheduling treatments when you have a day or two for recovery.

When will I see results?

You may notice improved skin texture within a week, but the most dramatic results appear gradually over 2-6 months as new collagen forms. Results continue to improve with each treatment in your series.

How many treatments do I need?

We recommend 3 treatments spaced 4-6 weeks apart for optimal results. Some patients may benefit from additional treatments depending on their concerns and goals.

Is it safe for all skin types?

Yes! Unlike some laser treatments, Skin Pen is safe and effective for all skin types and tones, with minimal risk of hyperpigmentation.

How long do results last?

With proper skincare and sun protection, results can last for many months. Many patients return for maintenance treatments 1-2 times per year to sustain their results.
