Key Ingredients We Recommend

Our product selection features scientifically proven ingredients known for their effectiveness:

  • Retinoids (Retinol, Tretinoin): Gold standard for anti-aging and acne treatment
  • Vitamin C: Powerful antioxidant that brightens and protects
  • Hyaluronic Acid: Superior hydrator that plumps and moisturizes
  • Peptides: Building blocks that support collagen production
  • Niacinamide: Multi-tasking ingredient for pores, tone, and texture
  • Alpha Hydroxy Acids (AHAs): Exfoliants that reveal smoother, brighter skin
  • Growth Factors: Support skin repair and regeneration
  • Antioxidants: Protect against environmental damage and aging

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between medical-grade and drugstore skincare?

Medical-grade products contain higher concentrations of active ingredients and are formulated to penetrate deeper into the skin. They're clinically tested and proven effective, whereas over-the-counter products often have lower concentrations that may not deliver significant results.

How do I know which products are right for me?

During your consultation, we'll assess your skin type, concerns, and goals to create a personalized product regimen. We consider factors like sensitivity, current skincare routine, and compatibility with any treatments you're receiving.

Can I use these products with my current skincare?

We'll review your current products and recommend how to integrate medical-grade skincare into your routine. Some products work well together, while others should be used separately to avoid irritation.

When will I see results?

Most patients notice improvements in skin texture and radiance within 2-4 weeks. More significant changes in fine lines, hyperpigmentation, and overall skin quality typically appear after 8-12 weeks of consistent use.

Do I need a consultation to purchase products?

Yes, we require a brief consultation to ensure you receive products appropriate for your skin. This helps prevent adverse reactions and ensures you're investing in products that will actually work for your specific needs.
