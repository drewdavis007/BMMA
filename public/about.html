About Us

Where beauty meets wellness

Our Mission

At Beautiful Minds Medical Aesthetics, we believe that true beauty radiates from within. Our mission is to help you achieve your aesthetic goals while prioritizing your overall health and well-being.

We understand that looking good and feeling good are deeply connected. That's why we've created a medical spa that focuses on both your mental and physical health, providing comprehensive care that addresses the whole person—not just your appearance.

"True health starts from within the body and reflects on the outside. Our mission is to improve our patients' health from the inside out."

Meet Our Founder and Nurse Practitioner!

Kayla Collum Davis

DNP, FNP-C

Kayla Collum Davis is the Founder of Beautiful Minds Medical Aesthetics. She graduated from the University of Mississippi Medical Center in 2019 with her Bachelor of Science in Nursing. She then worked in multiple nursing specialties in the hospital setting while completing her Doctorate of Nurse Practitioner degree in Family Medicine at the University of Alabama at Birmingham in 2023. She has always been passionate about medical healthcare as well as cosmetic aesthetics and skin care, so she created a clinic that incorporated both.

Beautiful Minds Medical Aesthetics is a medical spa that focuses on our patients' mental and physical health. True health starts from within the body and reflects on the outside. Our mission is to improve our patients' health from the inside out.

Every journey begins with a thorough consultation. We take the time to understand your concerns, goals, and medical history to create a treatment plan that's right for you.

We believe in empowering our patients with knowledge. We'll explain your treatment options, what to expect, and how to maintain your results for the long term.
