Neurotoxin Treatments

Reduce fine lines and achieve a smoother, more youthful appearance

What Are Neurotoxins?

Neurotoxin treatments are one of the most popular non-surgical cosmetic procedures available today. These injectable treatments work by temporarily relaxing facial muscles that cause wrinkles, resulting in smoother, younger-looking skin. At Beautiful Minds Medical Aesthetics, we offer several premium neurotoxin options to meet your unique needs and aesthetic goals.

Daxxify

Pricing

$8/unit

Daxxify is the newest FDA-approved neurotoxin, known for its long-lasting results. Clinical studies have shown that Daxxify can last up to 6 months or longer in some patients, making it an excellent choice for those seeking extended results from their treatments.

Benefits of Daxxify:

  • Longer-lasting results (up to 6+ months)
  • Smooth, natural-looking outcome
  • Fewer appointments needed throughout the year
  • Effective for moderate to severe frown lines
  • Peptide-powered formula for enhanced stability

Jeuveau

Pricing

$12/unit

Jeuveau, often called "Newtox," is a modern neurotoxin specifically designed for aesthetic use. It's highly effective at treating moderate to severe frown lines and other dynamic wrinkles.

Benefits of Jeuveau:

  • Fast-acting results (visible within 2-3 days)
  • Smooth, natural appearance
  • Specifically formulated for cosmetic use
  • Results typically last 3-4 months
  • Excellent for frown lines, forehead lines, and crow's feet

Dysport

Pricing

$12/unit

Dysport is a well-established neurotoxin known for its natural-looking results and quick onset of action. It has a slightly different formulation that allows it to spread more easily, making it ideal for treating larger areas.

Benefits of Dysport:

  • Fast results (often visible within 24-48 hours)
  • Natural, refreshed appearance
  • Excellent for treating larger areas like the forehead
  • Results last approximately 3-4 months
  • Softer, diffused effect for a natural look

Lip Flip

Pricing

$80

The lip flip is a subtle neurotoxin treatment that relaxes the muscles around the upper lip, causing it to gently "flip" outward for a fuller appearance without the use of dermal fillers.

Benefits of Lip Flip:

  • Subtle, natural-looking enhancement
  • No added volume (perfect for those who want subtle change)
  • Reduces gummy smile appearance
  • Quick, minimally invasive procedure
  • Results last approximately 2-3 months
  • Can be combined with lip filler for enhanced results

Therapeutic Neurotoxin Treatments

Beyond cosmetic applications, neurotoxins have proven therapeutic benefits for various medical conditions:

Botox for Chronic Migraines

$2,600

FDA-approved treatment for chronic migraine sufferers. Involves multiple injection sites around the head and neck to prevent migraine headaches before they start.

Alternative: Jeuveau at $12/unit for full or partial treatment

Treatment for Hyperhidrosis (Excessive Sweating)

Botox: $13/unit

Effectively reduces excessive sweating in underarms, hands, feet, or face by temporarily blocking sweat gland signals.

Alternatives:
Jeuveau or Dysport: $12/unit
Daxxify: $8/unit

TMJ Treatment

Botox: $13/unit

Relieves jaw tension, teeth grinding (bruxism), and TMJ-related pain by relaxing the masseter muscles.

Alternatives:
Jeuveau: $12/unit
Daxxify: $8/unit

What to Expect

Before Your Treatment

  • Avoid blood-thinning medications and supplements (aspirin, ibuprofen, vitamin E, fish oil) for 1 week prior
  • Avoid alcohol for 24 hours before treatment
  • Arrive with a clean face (no makeup)
  • Discuss your goals and concerns during your consultation

During Your Treatment

  • Treatment takes approximately 10-20 minutes
  • Minimal discomfort (most describe it as tiny pinpricks)
  • No anesthesia required
  • You can return to normal activities immediately

After Your Treatment

  • Avoid touching or rubbing treated areas for 4 hours
  • Remain upright for 4 hours after treatment
  • Avoid strenuous exercise for 24 hours
  • Results typically visible within 3-7 days
  • Full results develop over 2 weeks
  • Results last 3-6 months depending on the product used

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do results last?

Results vary by product: Daxxify can last up to 6+ months, while Botox, Jeuveau, and Dysport typically last 3-4 months. Individual results may vary based on metabolism, muscle strength, and lifestyle factors.

Is the treatment painful?

Most patients describe the sensation as a minor pinprick. The needles used are very fine, and the procedure is quick. Ice can be applied beforehand if you're concerned about discomfort.

Are there side effects?

Side effects are typically minimal and temporary. Some patients experience slight redness, swelling, or bruising at injection sites, which usually resolves within a few hours to days.

When will I see results?

Dysport works fastest (24-48 hours), Jeuveau shows results in 2-3 days, while Botox and Daxxify typically show results within 3-7 days. Full effects develop over 2 weeks.
