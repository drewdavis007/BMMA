What Are Dermal Fillers?

Dermal fillers are injectable gel-like substances that restore lost volume, smooth lines, soften creases, and enhance facial contours. At Beautiful Minds Medical Aesthetics, we use premium RHA (Resilient Hyaluronic Acid) fillers to provide natural-looking, long-lasting results that move with your face.

RHA Dermal Fillers

$650/syringe

RHA fillers are the latest generation of hyaluronic acid fillers, specifically designed to adapt to your facial movements for incredibly natural results.

What Makes RHA Fillers Different?

RHA fillers represent a breakthrough in dermal filler technology. Unlike traditional fillers, RHA is made using a gentle manufacturing process that preserves longer hyaluronic acid chains. This unique structure allows RHA to mimic the natural hyaluronic acid in your skin more closely.

Key Benefits of RHA Fillers

  • Adapts to your facial expressions for natural movement
  • Long-lasting results (12-15 months in most patients)
  • Smooth, natural-looking enhancement
  • Minimal downtime
  • Reversible if needed
  • FDA-approved for dynamic wrinkles and folds
  • Gradual, subtle improvement

What to Expect

Before Your Treatment

  • Schedule a consultation to discuss your goals and desired outcome
  • Avoid blood-thinning medications and supplements for 1 week prior (aspirin, ibuprofen, vitamin E, fish oil)
  • Avoid alcohol for 24 hours before treatment
  • Arrive with a clean face (no makeup in treatment area)
  • Consider scheduling treatment at least 2 weeks before any major events

During Your Treatment

  • Treatment typically takes 30-60 minutes depending on areas treated
  • Numbing cream may be applied for comfort
  • RHA fillers contain lidocaine for additional comfort during injection
  • Multiple injection points may be used for optimal results
  • You'll see immediate improvement in volume and contour

After Your Treatment

  • Mild swelling and bruising are normal and typically resolve within 3-7 days
  • Avoid touching or massaging treated areas for 24 hours
  • Sleep with head elevated for the first night
  • Avoid strenuous exercise for 24-48 hours
  • Avoid alcohol and excessive heat (saunas, hot tubs) for 24 hours
  • Apply ice to reduce swelling if needed
  • Final results visible once swelling subsides (1-2 weeks)
  • Results typically last 12-15 months

Why Choose RHA Over Other Fillers?

Natural Movement

RHA fillers are specifically engineered to stretch and compress with your facial expressions, ensuring you look natural when you smile, laugh, and talk. Traditional fillers can sometimes look stiff or unnatural in dynamic areas.

Longer-Lasting Results

The resilient structure of RHA fillers means they last longer than many traditional hyaluronic acid fillers, giving you beautiful results for 12-15 months on average.

Immediate, Natural-Looking Results

You'll see improvement immediately after treatment, with final results appearing once any minor swelling subsides. The results look and feel completely natural.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many syringes will I need?

This varies based on your individual goals and the areas being treated. During your consultation, we'll create a customized treatment plan. Some patients achieve their desired results with one syringe, while others may need 2-3 syringes for optimal enhancement.

Does it hurt?

Most patients describe the sensation as a slight pinch or pressure. We use numbing cream before treatment, and RHA fillers contain lidocaine for added comfort during injection.

When will I see results?

You'll see immediate improvement in volume and contour right after treatment. Final results are visible once any minor swelling subsides, typically within 1-2 weeks.

How long do results last?

RHA fillers typically last 12-15 months, though individual results may vary based on factors like metabolism, lifestyle, and the area treated.

Can fillers be reversed?

Yes, hyaluronic acid fillers like RHA can be dissolved using an enzyme called hyaluronidase if needed. This is one of the safety advantages of HA-based fillers.

Can I combine fillers with other treatments?

Absolutely! Fillers work beautifully with neurotoxins (Botox, Jeuveau, etc.) and other aesthetic treatments. Many patients opt for a combination approach for comprehensive facial rejuvenation.
